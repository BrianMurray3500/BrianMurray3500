Guidelines for Study Names and Descriptions

The name and description of your user research studies are displayed to participants if they display the study-link in their browser. The name and description should help the participants to understand what the study is about, and why they are being asked to participate.

Study Name

When you click Create A Study on the Research and Feedback page the Name and Description dialog is displayed.

The name is displayed to study participants so they can get an initial idea of what the study is about. You can use up to 40 characters for the study name.

In user research studies about the design of an application you could use the name of the application as the study name. In general, try to avoid using abbreviations as they may cause confusion.

  • Sample Good Name - Manage Payment

  • Sample Bad Name - Blocks SFIN GLAP
