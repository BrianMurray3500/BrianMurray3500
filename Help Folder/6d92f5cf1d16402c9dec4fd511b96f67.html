Create User Research Studies

To create a user research study you must first create a Build project, and then create the study within that project. In the project, you create the user research study by adding images and ask questions about those images. Then you publish the study to generate a study-link, which you can send to third parties by email.

Context

You must have created a project or be a member of an existing project in order an create a user research study.

Procedure

  1. Click the Home button on the navigation bar.

    Your project home page is displayed

  2. Click the tile for the project that you want to create a user research study about.

    In Build 1.0 the Research and Feedback page is displayed. In future versions of Build, the Prototype page is displayed, and you must click the Research button on the navigation bar to display the Research and Feedback page.

  3. Click the Create New Study button.

    The Study Name and Description dialog is displayed.

  4. Enter a name and description for the study.

    For more information, see Guidelines for Study Names and Descriptions

  5. Click the Save button.
  6. Add images to your research study by doing one of the following :
    • Click Images and browse your local environment to add images to your research study.
    • Click Snapshot to take and add a snapshot of all the pages in the prototype applications. Pages added as snapshots are not interactive for participants in your research study.
    • Click HTML prototype and browse your local environment to add all the pages of a HTML prototype application to your study. Pages added as part of a prototype are interactive for participants in your research study.

    For more information, see Guidelines for Adding Images and Prototypes

    Thumbnails of the images you have added are displayed in the research study home page.

  7. Delete pages you do not want to include in the research study by moving the pointer over the thumbnail and clicking the delete icon.
  8. Add a question about each image by moving the pointer over the thumbnail and clicking the question icon.

    For more informtion, see Guidelines for Adding Questions

    The Questions for this screen dialog is displayed.

  9. Enter a question about the image.
  10. Click + Add a question if you want to ask another question about this image.
  11. Click the Save and Next button to save your questions and move to the next image.
  12. Click Save and Close when you have added at least one quesiton for each image.
  13. Click the Preview icon to preview your study.

    Previewing your research study opens a new tab displays the research study exactly as participants in the study will see it. You can answer questions about images in a preview and the answers are not saved.

  14. Publish the study by clicking the Publish button.

Results

A unique study-link URL is displayed for your research study. You can invite thrid parties to participate in your research study by emailing them this study-link URL.
