View and Analyze Response to Research Studies

You can view participants' response to your research studies almost as soon as they enter them in their browser. A tile for each user research study created for the project is displayed on the Research and Feedback page.

You can view responses to studies displayed on the Active tab or the Archived tab. On each research tile the number of participants who have responded and the the total number of comments icons dropped on the images in the research study are displayed.

You click the research study tile to view the responses. Depending on your question, the positioning of the comment icons be significant and provide useful informaiton. For further information, click on each comment icon on the image to read comments entered by particpants.
