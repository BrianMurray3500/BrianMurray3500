Guidelines for Adding Images and Prototypes

You can include images in user research studies by uploading static images from your local environment, taking snapshots of single or multiple pages from your prototype application, or uploading Norman or Axure interactive HTML prototype applications.

Adding Static Image

Once you have entered a name and description for a research study, you must add one or more images. You can add images to your study as follows:

  • Click the UPLOAD button on the Image tile to upload images from your project Docs folder to the research, or to add images from your local environment to your project Docs folder and then upload them to the research study.
  • Click the SELECT button Snapshot tile to take an up-to-date snap-shot of all the pages in the prototype application. Once you have uploaded the snap-shot images to your reserach study, you can then delete any pages you do not want to include. Snap-shots of pages from your prototype can not be viewed in interactive mode in your research study.
  • Click the UPLOAD ZIP button on the HTML prototypes tile to browse for and upload a compressed (.ZIP) HTML Norman or Axure prototype application to your research study.Once you have uploaded an entire HTML protoype to your reserach study, you can then delete any pages you do not want to include in the reserach study. Pages from your prototype uploaded to your research study using the UPLOAD ZIP button can be viewed in interactive mode by participants of your research study.
  • Click the Upload button to browse and upload images from your local environment to the research study.
  • Click the Select from Assets button to upload images from your project Docs folder to the research, or to add images from your local environment to your project Docs folder and then upload them to the user research study.
Note

If you have not published your user research study, but have already added images, a snap-shot, or a HTML prototype application, you can click the Upload or Select from Assets buttons to add additional images.
