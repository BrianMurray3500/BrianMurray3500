Pause, Archive, and Restart Research Studies

You your pause or archive your research studies. Particpants cannot respond to paused or archived studies. You can restart paused or archived studies whenever you want.

Pausing Studies

You pause an active study by opening the study from the Active tab on the Research and Feedback page, and clicking the Pause on the results page of the research study. You may, for example, want to pause a study before you begin to analyse the responses.

Once you have paused a study, the Archive and Restart buttons become available in the research study. It is not possible to retrieve the study link for paused studies from the research study.
Note

Paused studies are displayed from the Paused tab on the Research and Feedback page.

Archiving Studies

You can archive paused studies by clicking the Archive button on the study page of a Paused study. It is not possible to retrieve the study link for archived studies from the research study.
Note

Archived studies are displayed from the Paused tab on the Research and Feedback page.

Restarting Studies

You restart paused and archived studies by clicking the Restart button on the results page of paused or archived research studies. Once a research study is restarted, it is possible to retrive the study-link for the research study.
